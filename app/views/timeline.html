HWHC timeline

2022

Team 1 frontend reviews

May 2022

Completed reviews of 4 services; Am I Exempt, Matex Register, Matex Apply, PPC Online.

Reviewed each service checking for accessibility, best practices, performance, security and SEO.

Used tools such as Google lighthouse to complete the performance tests

Notes

  • May 2022 - frontend reviews

Additional

May 2022

An update to Chrome changed impacted the timeout model on PPC Online. It was not possible to click the 'Continue' button in Chrome.

Fixed by upgrading to v0.3 of the session-timout model and moving the timeout HTML to render under the footer.

Tidy up

April - May 2022

Picking up small accessibility fixes to help services reach WCAG AA compliance.

Conducting WAVE feature reviews at the request of the test team.

We have branches where we've converted Matex Apply, Matex Register and PPC from the NHSBSA elements toolkit to the NHSUK toolkit. The conversions were completed months ago, but the branches have not been tested or deployed. We merged in latest updates from those projects to ensure the branches are ready for testing when required.

Working on small tickets such as upgrading the timeout component for LIS and adding dynamic partner error messages on Eligibility Checker.

Notes

  • Accessible modal checklist

Speech recognition fixes

February - March 2022

Completed speech recognition fixes across all HWHC services.

Used Apple's Voice Control to test bug fixes locally.

Most fixes involved making hidden labels visible.

Notes

  • List of useful Voice Control commands
  • Most of the bugs were raised when using Voice Control. Dragon is a lot more intuitive and seemed to work well with exisiting services.

Speech recognition fixes for Eligibility checker

Complete fixes for bugs raised by our testers during speech recognition testing.

Notes

Document Manager - Accessibility fixes

March - May 2021

Asked by the business to complete accessibility fixes on Document manager.

Notes

Eligibility Checker (EGC)

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

The Eligibility checker is a service that allows users to see if they get help to pay these NHS charges, such as: NHS prescriptions, travel to receive NHS treatment, etc

You can access this locally at: http://localhost:8080/check-for-help-paying-nhs-costs/start.

Low income scheme (LIS)

Toolkit version: Elements v1.0.22954

Description

If you have a low income, the NHS Low Income Scheme could help you pay for medical costs such as; NHS prescription charges, NHS dental treatment charges, etc.

You can access this locally at: http://localhost:10050/apply-for-help-with-nhs-costs/apply-online.

Notes

As of November 2021 there are 339 pages in the LIS application. The pages are created using YAML, which call HTML/Thymeleaf fragments.

As of May 2022 LIS has upgraded to Java 11.

Assessor view

The assessor view is built into LIS and is used by a team of assessors at Bridge house, who get a play back of applications in their own dashboard view.

The check your answers screens the users sees are played back to the assessors in the assessor view. It's the same HTML, but we use custom CSS to change the display in the assessor view.s

It's on the roadmap to decouple the assessor view from LIS.

You can access this locally at: http://localhost:10050/apply-for-help-with-nhs-costs/sign-in.

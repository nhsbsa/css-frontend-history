Student services

Student Bursaries (SB) Student

Toolkit version: NHSUK v4.1.0

The NHS offers bursaries to help with the cost of tuition and living expenses for certain healthcare courses. Eligible students include those studying nursing, midwifery, and certain allied health professions.

The bursary amount varies depending on the course and the student's financial situation.

SB Student is a public facing service for students to register and apply for bursaries.

You can access this locally at: http://localhost:8081/nhssb/

Social Work Bursary (SWB) Student

Toolkit version: NHSUK v4.1.0

Users can manage their Social Work Bursary by signing up for a MyFunding account. Through their account they can complete applications, check enorlment statuses and view bursary payments.

You can access this locally at: http://localhost:8081/myfunding/
